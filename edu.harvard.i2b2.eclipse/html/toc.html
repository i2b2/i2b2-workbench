i2b2 Workbench Help

The i2b2 Workbench is an application that gives users a tool to interact with different parts of the i2b2 Hive. The most basic workbench consists of a login view that connects to the Project Management (PM) Cell. Additional plug-ins connect to other i2b2 cells providing different functions to the user. Refer to plug-in help documents for information on each individual plug-in.
